# WGInvite-Master

WGInvite Master v3.7

Build: Stable v3.7 for WotLK 3.3.5

WGInvite Master is the definitive World of Warcraft 3.3.5 addon for raid leaders,
multiboxers, and Wintergrasp enthusiasts. It combines heavy-duty automation with
a modern "Glass UI" display to streamline the recruitment, queueing, and
management of large-scale PvP groups.

Core Features

1. Wintergrasp Automation
Auto-Join Queue: Instantly clicks "Join Battle" when the Wintergrasp queue
window appears. Never miss a battle again while tabbed out or managing alts.
Glass UI Timer: A high-performance, on-screen timer.
Resizable & Scalable: Font size adjusts dynamically as you stretch the
window.
Smart Color: Turns RED when <15 minutes remain.
Locking: Right-click the timer window to lock it in place.

2. Intelligent Recruitment
Multi-Keyword Whisper: Auto-invite players based on customizable
keywords (e.g., inv, wg, invite ).
Mass Invites: Dedicated buttons to invite your entire Guild (filtered by rank/
level) or your Friends List with one click.
Auto-Raid Convert: Automatically upgrades your party to a Raid group when
the 6th member joins.

3. Management & Security
The Nuke (Disband): Clear your entire raid and leave the group with a single
confirmed action.
Auto-Assistant: Define a list of names that will be automatically promoted to
Assistant status upon joining.
Advanced Blacklist: Prevents specific players from using your auto-invite
keywords. Includes a right-click context menu integration on player portraits.

4. QoL Enhancements
Auto-Accept: Instantly accept group invites from Guildies or Friends.
Duel Blocker: Automatically declines all duel requests.
Auto-Release: Spirit-releases automatically in Battlegrounds and
Wintergrasp.

Installation

Download the WGInvite folder.
Place it in your World of Warcraft/Interface/AddOns/ directory.
Ensure you have the Ace3 library suite installed or included in the Libs
folder.

Commands
/wgi - Open the Configuration GUI
/wginvite - Alternative settings command
Right-Click - Click Timer Window to Lock/Unlock
R-Click Icon - Instant Mass Guild Invite
